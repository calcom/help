---
title: "Adding Zoom as the Meeting Location for Your Event Type"
---

To add Zoom as the meeting location for your event type, you need to follow these steps:

- Install the [Zoom](https://app.cal.com/apps/zoom) app in our app store
- Go to the **Basics** settings of your event type
- Select Zoom Video in the Location dropdown
- Once selected, click **Save** in the top right corner of the UI

That's it!
